<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2021/22时尚九大潮流趋势（上）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442483-30d22904ef125e1fdf157af9690a8a02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2021年流行的波西米亚风、学院风、撞色拼搭、阔腿裤、大西装、休闲风衣等在2022年会继续流行。（Getty Images／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月17日讯】（大纪元记者李平综合报导）在过去超过半个世纪的时间里，“最新 ”的<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A%E6%BD%AE%E6%B5%81.md#1">时尚潮流</a>基本都是从T台流出，藉由媒体传播。如今，几乎全民都在参与时尚的诠释和展示，社交媒体、街头服饰时尚以及名人等都在演绎着时尚的新鲜与活力。</p>
<p>2020年时尚的中性风、2020~2021年的怪诞风，都给2021~2022两年趋于时尚主流的态势铺垫了更大的市场空间。</p>
<p>无论从办公室的<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E5%8B%A4%E8%A3%85.md#1">通勤装</a>，到<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E5%85%B8%E6%B4%BE%E5%AF%B9.md#1">庆典派对</a>的节日服饰或外出旅行的<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>，以下9种潮流趋势都能为你提供时尚、创新、经济、易操作的穿搭参考，大家一起来看看吧。</p>
<h2>1.带帽衫+西装外套</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13442573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442573" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442573-GettyImages-1325437317.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442573-GettyImages-1325437317-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442573" class="wp-caption-text">灰蓝色扎染带帽衫，外套深蓝色西装，沉稳中带活泼。法国巴黎时装周2022春夏装秀场。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>撇开其遥远的中世纪历史前身，带帽衫在美国最早是供给户外工人保暖的工作装，后来演变成街头叛逆形象的代言。历经文化抗争和洗白，转为大众服饰，被称作北美“标配”服装。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442496-GettyImages-1340582558.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442496-GettyImages-1340582558-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442496" class="wp-caption-text">德国柏林的2021“关于你”时装周秀场。深驼色带帽衫与同色裤装，外搭白色夹克。（Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当前<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A%E6%BD%AE%E6%B5%81.md#1">时尚潮流</a>中最流行的一种风格就是带帽衫，穿着舒适，具有特色。带帽衫百变百搭，西装外套配带帽衫不再是巷尾街头的另类风格，而已跻身主流造型。一上身，似乎年龄、职业、阶层马上就没了界限。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442454" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442454-GettyImages-1306486833.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442454-GettyImages-1306486833-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442454" class="wp-caption-text"><br />米色带帽衫，同色系运动裤，细格大外套，休闲与正装融合出别致的<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>。休闲2021墨尔本时装节T台晚会秀场。（Mackenzie Sweetnam/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>业界建议：带帽衫配超大西装效果奇好。薄款带帽衫可以搭配较为合身的西装外套。无论怎么穿都不会出错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442452-GettyImages-1281745402.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13442452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442452-GettyImages-1281745402-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442452" class="wp-caption-text">深灰色西服套装配搭黑色短款马甲灰色带帽衫，浑然一体，既酷又帅的造型。韩国汉城时装周2021SS秀场。（Justin Shin/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>可以选一件修长的锥形带帽衫，最好是较深的纯色，不要有太多刺绣或图案，搭配一件纯色的深色西装外套，可依据场合不同搭配休闲裤或牛仔裤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442450" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442450-GettyImages-1204355436.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442450-GettyImages-1204355436-600x938.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="704" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442450" class="wp-caption-text">格子西服套装搭配带帽衫，同色系仿皮草外套，任潇洒与帅气飞扬。纽约时装周秀场。（Albert Urso/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2. 波西米亚花饰风</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13442457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442457" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442457-GettyImages-1344323770.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442457-GettyImages-1344323770-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442457" class="wp-caption-text">金、褐色麦穗与红色印花相衬，有如花瓣雨混杂金色的麦穗洒落人间，让人充满浪漫遐思；肤色的及地长裙薄纱般质地，肌肤若隐若现，飘然若仙。巴黎时装周2022春夏时装女装秀场。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13442473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442473-GettyImages-1354614276.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13442473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442473-GettyImages-1354614276-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442473" class="wp-caption-text">米色无肩带长裙，点缀着各色野花，平跟凉鞋，显示出女性精致柔美，传达与对美和自然的向往与热爱。2021 年墨尔本时装周闭幕秀场。（Quinn Rooney/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13442476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442476" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442476-GettyImages-1357667746.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442476-GettyImages-1357667746-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442476" class="wp-caption-text">香奈儿经典圆领外套设计，加上2022流行的新元素——<ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E8%A5%BF%E7%B1%B3%E4%BA%9A%E9%A3%8E.md#1">波西米亚风</a>与哥特风黑色结合，经典又时尚。法国巴黎12月7日举办2021/2022香奈儿专业艺术时装周秀场。 (Photo by Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13442459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442459" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442459-GettyImages-1350993146.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442459-GettyImages-1350993146-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442459" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/dlgkam388/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E8%A5%BF%E7%B1%B3%E4%BA%9A%E9%A3%8E.md#1">波西米亚风</a>与西部牛仔及学院元素结合。2021 年 11 月在加利福尼亚州洛杉矶举行的“古奇爱之炫展”时装秀长。 (Photo by Emma McIntyre/Getty Images for Gucci)</figcaption></figure>
<p>波西米亚风（Bohemian/Boho Style）可谓是时尚界中的长青风格，几乎季季受青睐，甚至连续几季流行于潮流之巅。层叠的蕾丝、民族风印花、皮质的流苏、褶皱的裙摆、刺绣、珠串和平底鞋，都是波希米亚风格的经典元素。</p>
<p>波西米亚风吸收了多地的民族风元素，在生活和艺术上脱离世俗常规、追求自由、强调个人风格。波希米亚不仅代表流行风格，更是各种自由洒脱、热情奔放、色彩鲜艳、繁复炫酷的代名词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442451" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442451-GettyImages-1257654569.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442451-GettyImages-1257654569-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442451" class="wp-caption-text">素色钩花头巾配全身米色怀旧细花纹连身裙，金花饰项链，配棕色短靴。文静素雅又充满个性。意大利莱切的2021迪奥游弋时装秀。 （Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>时尚趋势：</strong>本季设计师不再对复古服装和玫瑰印花感兴趣，而是将花色图案与哥特式黑色、田园风、深色学院风（暗学院风）、浅色学院风（轻学院风）更多结合。</p>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>一般可以选用局部波西米亚风，其余采用纯色搭配；如果搭配得当，多种不同图案的印花拼搭，也会造成多彩丰富的视觉效果。避免选用枯萎玫瑰的印花，选取更为鲜亮、更富幻想的花型。比如将亮黄色、鲜绿色与红色，黑色混合的图案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442456" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442456-GettyImages-1327258300.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442456-GettyImages-1327258300-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442456" class="wp-caption-text">两种图案的印花拼搭。2021/2022巴黎时装周高级定制时装秀场。（Kristy Sparow/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>3/9 撞色相搭</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13442475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442475-GettyImages-1357667735.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442475-GettyImages-1357667735-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442475" class="wp-caption-text">黑色挺括上装，配浅蓝牛仔阔腿裤，裤脚荷叶飞边，让牛仔布料显得精致高雅。12月7日法国巴黎2021/2022香奈儿专业艺术时装周秀场，位于香奈儿新总部大楼Le 19M。 (Photo by Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>撞色穿搭可以让你在芸芸人海中辨识度大增，瞬间即可脱颖而出。原理是采用两种或多种反差大的颜色搭配造成视觉冲击，既可是对比色、互补色，比如红与绿、湖蓝与橘黄，也可是色相环上相邻色的拼搭，比如深粉与红色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442474" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442474-GettyImages-1357665475.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442474-GettyImages-1357665475-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442474" class="wp-caption-text">香奈儿经典格纹套装，明暗纹理、色彩的碰撞恰到好处。2021/2022香奈儿专业艺术时装周秀场。香奈儿在其新总部大楼Le 19M发布了第20届专业艺术时装系列。 (Photo by Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>拼搭法则：</strong>在外形、色彩搭配上最好保持简单的原则。一组对比色如果颜色配比、浓度、明度、冷暖、面积完全相同，会造成视觉不适，可采用的局部色块，也可以主色加副色装饰拼搭，比如一种颜色的衣服以撞色的线缝制，或者主色衣服配撞色的围巾。撞色渐变也是一种让色彩搭配更为自然和谐的方式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442458" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442458-GettyImages-1349303791.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442458-GettyImages-1349303791-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442458" class="wp-caption-text">颜色、色彩明度、浓度的碰撞，却产生和谐有跳跃的美感。阿联酋2021“迪拜：只此一夜”乔治·阿玛尼时装秀场。（Cedric Ribeiro/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>外形简单的长裙、铅笔裙等配色可选择对比色；若喜欢雅淡色的穿搭，可采用莫兰蒂色系的撞色，更为时尚典雅。粉红色与黄色、橙色与蓝色或绿色与黑色等颜色的碰撞，可以打造出一些最有效的视觉效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442507-GettyImages-1303873350.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442507-GettyImages-1303873350-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442507" class="wp-caption-text">裙子色块的撞色拼搭。让整个连身裙看起来活力四射。2021/2022米兰时装周秋冬时装秀。（Jacopo M. Raule/Getty Images for Maryling）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13442449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442449" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442449-GettyImages-461829164.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442449-GettyImages-461829164-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442449" class="wp-caption-text">浅色系撞色拼搭。温柔、淡雅、和谐。梅赛德斯-奔驰时装周柏林秋冬时装秀场。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images for Marc Cain）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>4.厚底徒步靴/拖拉机靴</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13442497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442497" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442497-GettyImages-1340582645.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442497-GettyImages-1340582645-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442497" class="wp-caption-text">柔和的莫兰蒂蓝连身裙装，同色围巾，同色系羽绒外套，搭黑色厚底靴，黑色细皮带显示腰线，有呼应了鞋子和头发的颜色。雅致休闲不失可爱。柏林“关于你”时装周2021秋冬时装秀。（Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圆头、厚实坚固的鞋底，夸张的厚粗鞋底纹路有如拖拉机轮胎，这款看似不起眼的拖拉机靴，却受到很多品牌和零售商的青睐，设计师们似乎很容易从这款鞋子中获取设计和搭配灵感，它自然也成为今年最受欢迎的时尚潮流之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13442509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13442509" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442509-GettyImages-1324054808.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13442509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13442509-GettyImages-1324054808-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13442509" class="wp-caption-text">黑白相间的加长卫衣，配上黑色厚底袜靴，青春活力四射。伦敦2021毕业时装周秀场。（Joe Maher/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>单品优点：</strong>鞋底厚实，脚不易累，腿和脚更易保持干燥；具增高效果及拉伸小腿线条。</p>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>如果想获得实用、随性休闲的外观，可以用拖拉机靴搭配休闲运动裤或紧身裤；靴子搭配长裙或超短裙，搭配长袍或细长毛衣，打底裤看起来很好看；与牛仔裤也能结成强强联盟。</p>
