<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>经典又新潮 开衫的7种穿搭法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561982-FotoJet-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>开衫是胶囊衣橱中必备的单品，百搭各种服饰，可打造不同造型风格。（Getty Images／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年02月08日讯】（大纪元记者李平综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%A1%AB.md#1">开衫</a>是男士、女士胶囊衣橱的必备单品，不仅因为它<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>流行，更因为其款式多样且超级百搭，一年四季都可以穿着。今天我们就来谈一谈本季<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%A1%AB.md#1">开衫</a>如何<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>更为时尚应季。</p>
<p>开衫英文是cardigan，命名缘由来自卡迪根伯爵七世<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A9%B9%E5%A7%86%E6%96%AF%E2%80%A7%E5%B8%83%E9%B2%81%E5%BE%B7%E5%86%85%E5%B0%94.md#1">詹姆斯?布鲁德内尔</a>（James Brudenell）。传说，当年这位英国陆军少将的长大衣下摆不小心被火烧着后，就此发明了短款的开衫，卡迪根在战斗胜利后名声鹊起，开衫也随之流行开来。</p>
<p>中文翻译为开衫，顾名思义，就是前方有开口的毛衫，穿脱起来比套头的毛衣要方便很多，因此早年很受军人、工人、运动员等的青睐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13561986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561986" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561986-GettyImages-1137335913.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561986-GettyImages-1137335913-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561986" class="wp-caption-text">经典的男装开衫，小翻领、经典花纹，配深色高领衫。梅赛德斯-奔驰伊斯坦布尔时装秀场。（Ferhat Zupcevic/Getty Images for IMG）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开衫的灵感来自19世纪英国男性军官所穿的马甲，因此在一段很长时间，它是男士的专属服装。开衫走入女士<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>还得归功于可可·香奈儿（Coco Chanel）在海边首次穿着。</p>
<p>早期的开衫为保暖透气都是以天然羊毛织物为主，后来演变为合成纤维，不同种类的植物产生不同的感受和舒适度，而打造出不同的<ahref="https://github.com/vcziuk324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13561989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561989" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561989-GettyImages-1276458769.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561989-GettyImages-1276458769-600x901.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561989" class="wp-caption-text">轻薄透的开衫也是适合夏日的装着，罩在裙装之上，只系顶部的扣子，也不失为一种时尚的穿搭。米兰时装周2021春夏秀场。（Pietro S. D&#8217;Aprano/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开衫有高领、翻领、圆领、V领等各种领口，也有无袖、短袖、七分袖和长袖之分，适合与各种服饰搭配；天然质地的开衫温暖柔软，可以贴身穿着，也适合多层叠穿，比如可在衬衣、裙装上叠穿，也可以在夹克、西装内配搭。</p>
<h2>1. 超大、厚实开衫穿搭</h2>
<p>最简单的造型，就是简单的上衣、T恤外罩一件超大的开衫，配一条长裤和靴子。长的、超大的或厚实的开衫，在本季是流行趋势。厚大的开衫穿搭要注意显示腰线，可将下摆别到下装中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13562009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13562009" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13562009-GettyImages-1366437009.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13562009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13562009-GettyImages-1366437009-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13562009" class="wp-caption-text">模特身着羊毛彩色大开衫，内搭衬衣、高领衫，以腰带扎系，显示腰线。2022年1月18日法国巴黎时装周2022/2023秋冬男装秀场。（Kristy Sparow/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13561994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561994" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561994-GettyImages-1354162332.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561994-GettyImages-1354162332-600x856.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="643" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561994" class="wp-caption-text">紫线条的灰色长款羊毛开衫，成了莫德?阿帕托（Maude Apatow）的披肩，随意的搭在她精致的小裙装外。这是她在2021 年年底参加加州洛杉矶“Get Back”纪录片发行仪式上的装扮。（Rich Fury/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2. 短款开衫穿搭</h2>
<p>短款的开衫在本季依然流行，穿搭起来更凸显良好身材比例，与裙装、裤装，特别是阔腿裤，都可搭配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13561983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561983" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561983-GettyImages-464468396.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561983-GettyImages-464468396-600x902.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561983" class="wp-caption-text">短款夹克式开衫，米色小翻领、隐藏式拉链、袖子与下摆以米色收口，搭配系格纹过膝西装裙，高雅职业。米兰时装周Max Mara秀场。（Pietro D&#8217;Aprano/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13561987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561987" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561987-GettyImages-1229028742.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561987-GettyImages-1229028742-600x899.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561987" class="wp-caption-text">白色超短开衫配高腰罗卜裤，配踝靴。梅赛德斯-奔驰伊斯坦布尔时装秀场。（Ekrem Serif Egeli/Getty Images for IHKIB）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3. 与高领衫、圆领衫搭配</h2>
<p>开衫最常见、也是最易穿搭的就是与高领衫、圆领衫配搭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13561991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561991" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561991-GettyImages-1344923071.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561991-GettyImages-1344923071-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561991" class="wp-caption-text">粉色马甲内叠穿短款开衫叠穿。巴黎时装周2022春夏女装秀场。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>4. 与衬衣搭配</h2>
<p>在气温逐渐回升，但依旧乍暖还寒之时，可以选用一款长袖或无袖的长宽开衫，叠穿在衬衣外边，可着紧身打底裤，也可将开衫作为连身裙穿着。而开衫搭配衬衣裙也是不错的选择。</p>

Hekimian/Getty Images For Chanel）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>5. 与裙装搭配</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13561985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13561985" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561985-GettyImages-1030505060.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13561985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13561985-GettyImages-1030505060-600x941.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="706" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13561985" class="wp-caption-text">同色的穿搭永远都不会错，粉色的小开衫外套，配上粉色的连衣裙，黑色针织腰带凸显腰线，并与头发颜色呼应，非常经典。纽约时装周。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images for NYFW: The Shows）</figcaption></figure>
<p>开衫与裙子更是可以百搭出万种风情。可以选用中长款的开衫配皮裙，可以将开衫的下摆别到裙子中，显示高腰线及美好的身材比例。也可以在连身裙上叠穿开衫，这样可以让美丽飘逸的夏秋的裙装再次上身。</p>

<h2>6. 与裤装搭配</h2>
<p>开衫，不论大小长短都是可以很容易地与裤装拼搭。可采用阔腿裤、皮裤来营造时尚、前卫的形象；如果想略显正式，还可以选择正装西裤搭配短款蓬蓬袖开衫。</p>
