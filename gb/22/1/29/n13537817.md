<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>你知道吗？“寿司”最初原形 只吃鱼不吃饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/110407121956100445-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本美食─寿司（Fotolia ） 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">寿司</a>最早源于亚热带地区，大约在西元八世纪传入日本。早期没有冷藏设备，为了延长保存时间，人们就把鱼肉腌制后包进发酵过的米饭中防止腐坏，食用时不吃饭只吃鱼。这就是“寿司”最初的原形。</p>
<p>接着人们逐渐缩短发酵时间，把饭跟鱼一起吃；甚至直接省略发酵步骤，把醋拌入饭中，让饭带点微酸还能去腥。这种不需发酵、不需烹调的<ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">寿司</a>料理，省时又省事，对于生活忙碌的人（如东京地区）来说，非常受到欢迎，并逐渐流传到世界各地。</p>

<figure id="attachment_13538324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538324-130534817a5aea8140d4c5460c40febe.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538324-130534817a5aea8140d4c5460c40febe-450x294.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538324" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>寿司的种类</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13538322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538322-2ce113f73cf3a993fffeb1d80b5d7176.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538322-2ce113f73cf3a993fffeb1d80b5d7176-450x293.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538322" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<ul>
<li><strong><ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%B1%E6%8A%BC%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">箱押寿司</a> はこずし（或押寿司 押し寿司）</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>大阪流传已久的寿司，又叫做押寿司。在方型的木箱底层铺上生鱼片等配料，放入醋饭、盖上盖子用力压实，再把箱子倒过来，让食材在上面。装饰好即可切成片状食用。</p>
<ul>
<li><strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8F%A1%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">握寿司</a> 握り寿司</strong></strong>
<p><figure id="attachment_13538321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538321-907b372755d43f911ef7ba782be3dd05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538321-907b372755d43f911ef7ba782be3dd05-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538321" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure></li>
</ul>
<p>东京流传已久的寿司。用手把醋饭握成一口大小后，再铺上配料即完成。<ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8F%A1%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">握寿司</a>是现今最普遍的寿司类型。</p>
<ul>
<li><strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B7%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">卷寿司</a> 巻き寿司</strong></strong>
<p><figure id="attachment_13538320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538320" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538320-f4d666c95940d630763a5088c5d91b6d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538320-f4d666c95940d630763a5088c5d91b6d-450x529.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="529" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538320" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure></li>
</ul>
<p>在海苔上铺一层醋饭再盖上配料，卷成饭卷的形状。比较粗的称作“太<ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B7%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">卷寿司</a>”，细长的则是“细卷寿司”。还有用海苔围出空间放食材的“军舰卷（军舰巻き）”，也是卷寿司的一种。</p>
<ul>
<li><strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%A3%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">散寿司</a> 散らし寿司</strong></strong>
<p><figure id="attachment_13538323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538323-e3808dd6d91188f22ba8a1c4573b98b5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538323-e3808dd6d91188f22ba8a1c4573b98b5-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538323" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure></li>
</ul>
<p>日文的“散（ちらす）”，意思就是“发送到四处的传单”，所以在醋饭上散落着各种丰富配料制成的寿司，就称为“<ahref="https://github.com/eltbxu3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%A3%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">散寿司</a>”，外型上跟盖饭相似。<strong>?</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>享受寿司的秘诀</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong><span style="color: #000000;">品尝寿司的顺序</span></strong></li>
</ul>
<p>依据不同配料，建议先从口味清淡的开始，再吃到味道较浓郁的寿司，最后再吃甜点。另外，在品尝味道不同的寿司前，可以先吃点腌渍姜片清理口中的味道，更能好好感受下个寿司的美味。</p>
<p>白肉海鲜 比目鱼、鲷鱼、鱿鱼……等</p>
<p>红肉海鲜 鲑鱼、鲔鱼、虾……等</p>
<p>青背鱼 沙丁鱼、鲭鱼……等</p>
<p>腌渍海鲜或其他 腌渍虾子、玉子烧、军舰卷……等</p>
<ul>
<li><strong><span style="color: #000000;">沾酱方式</span></strong></li>
</ul>
<p>要把酱油沾在配料（ねた）上，而不是沾在醋饭（しゃり）上。因为饭吸附了酱油后，可能会盖过配料的味道。用筷子夹寿司的时候角度斜一点，比较容易把酱油沾在配料上。寿司本身已经有酱料的话，就可以省略沾酱的动作。</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>特殊的点菜方式</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p>如果是到高档的寿司店用餐，也可以选择无菜单料理（おまかせ）。“おまかせ（Omakase）”这日文的意思就是“委托”，意思是：“放心交给主厨吧！”⊙</p>
