<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2022年的第一场雪（2）鸥鸭冬日嬉戏图</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508334-3a159c3ee278a046e6eada67d54e537c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬日嬉戏图。罗城珍尼西河（Genesee River）畔的小公园里，聚集了一群绿头鸭与海鸥。2022年的一场初雪成了它们的嬉戏游乐场。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>一场久违的大雪，不仅是人们所期待的。秋日里贴好肥膘的飞禽们亦能在雪中戏耍的不亦乐乎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508330" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508330-556c30c6b87c5f516b67d9456277ec80.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508330-556c30c6b87c5f516b67d9456277ec80-450x284.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508330" class="wp-caption-text">小公园里的小池塘。（图片/<ahref="https://github.com/bpivcn3396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B5%B7%E4%BC%A6.md#1">周海伦</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bpivcn3396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%9F%8E%E7%8F%8D%E5%B0%BC%E8%A5%BF%E6%B2%B3.md#1">罗城珍尼西河</a>（Genesee River）畔的小公园里的一个小池塘，是一群<ahref="https://github.com/bpivcn3396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%A4%B4%E9%B8%AD.md#1">绿头鸭</a>与海鸥的聚集地。2022年一场初雪后，这里成了它们的冬日嬉戏游乐场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508335-a06c9b09cbd6684b4b0a5c3d74ea3fec.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13508335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508335-a06c9b09cbd6684b4b0a5c3d74ea3fec-450x611.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="611" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508335" class="wp-caption-text">冬林白雪与海鸥。（图片/<ahref="https://github.com/bpivcn3396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B5%B7%E4%BC%A6.md#1">周海伦</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海鸥与<ahref="https://github.com/bpivcn3396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%A4%B4%E9%B8%AD.md#1">绿头鸭</a>基本上各玩各的。海鸥们多聚集在岸边雪地里，而绿头鸭们则喜欢凫在水面上。大概这也是为什么“春江水暖鸭先知。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508345-fd88cc0f2ff1d5df9f3d96f6cad0af78.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13508345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508345-fd88cc0f2ff1d5df9f3d96f6cad0af78-450x565.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="565" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508345" class="wp-caption-text">雪中海鸥。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果不是眼睛是黑色的，翅膀上的羽毛是灰黑色的，嘴是黄的，一群海鸥蹲在雪地里还真不好分辨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508337" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508337-5a48c2b48e855e3d06f65e4350655f2c.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508337-5a48c2b48e855e3d06f65e4350655f2c-450x288.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508337" class="wp-caption-text">列队待飞。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我一直不知道鸟儿们是怎么沟通的。我也没听到什么号令，但只见一只只海鸥整齐的排好队，只等一声令下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508350" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508350-cc9b9cf18322345531319f3a3d79b524.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508350-cc9b9cf18322345531319f3a3d79b524-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508350" class="wp-caption-text">预备！。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>难道它就是号令官？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508343" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508343-deadf4135f48fe6bf3011e22b4b4aece.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508343-deadf4135f48fe6bf3011e22b4b4aece-450x266.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508343" class="wp-caption-text">飞！。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一声令下，上百只海鸥翩翩起舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508339-f7ee9b8b782669047e48ba8f83c4d9af.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13508339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508339-f7ee9b8b782669047e48ba8f83c4d9af-450x590.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508339" class="wp-caption-text">秀恩爱。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但也有不听号令的。两只热恋中的海鸥旁若无人的在大石头上秀恩爱。旁边那只直呼“辣眼睛！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508332" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508332-af4b9cf791e3d248db77bc00c582dc88.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13508332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508332-af4b9cf791e3d248db77bc00c582dc88-450x280.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508332" class="wp-caption-text">水上嬉戏的绿头鸭。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13508333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508333" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508333-17470af6e3eccdc9cab7d3112ddacbfb.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508333-17470af6e3eccdc9cab7d3112ddacbfb-450x311.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508333" class="wp-caption-text">水上嬉戏的绿头鸭。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>池塘的另一边则是绿头鸭的领地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508336" style="width: 591px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508336-e6805b1fb8bb0046f64e3c3440c6a7ee.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508336-e6805b1fb8bb0046f64e3c3440c6a7ee-450x307.jpg" alt="" width="591" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508336" class="wp-caption-text">共舞。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一般情况下海鸥与它们互不相关，但一旦有好吃的海鸥也会闯进来一起抢。</p>
<p>正好那天有个游人带了一袋面包来喂鸭。一场由一片面包引发的混战，精彩至极！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508367" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508367-6b8bc9279152ecdebeaef33a906c8afa.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508367-6b8bc9279152ecdebeaef33a906c8afa-450x285.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508367" class="wp-caption-text">虽千万鸭，吾往矣！（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>千军万鸭，为了一面面包，冲啊！领头的那只颇有“虽千万鸭，吾往矣！”之气概。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13508329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508329" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508329-eca661ae3f0dbacc02ebd2170048da2f.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508329-eca661ae3f0dbacc02ebd2170048da2f-450x284.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508329" class="wp-caption-text">一块面包引发的一场混战。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13508385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13508385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508385-46b2e9df50520bb32f15f7f14ecff0ee.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13508385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13508385-46b2e9df50520bb32f15f7f14ecff0ee-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13508385" class="wp-caption-text">开心的独舞。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
