<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一锅一餐 美味营养的豆腐鸡胸肉锅饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13553370-admin-ajax.php_-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豆腐鸡胸肉锅饭（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div><strong>编者按：</strong>来吃“<ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%85%E9%A5%AD.md#1">锅饭</a>”吧！在被时间追着跑、身心俱疲很想好好吃一顿饭时，只要将蔬菜、海鲜、肉类放入锅中炊煮，倒入快速<ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E6%B1%A4.md#1">高汤</a>及加入白米，不需要花费太多心思与时间，一锅集结营养和美味的丰盛料理，轻松上桌。</div>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">豆腐<ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a><ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%85%E9%A5%AD.md#1">锅饭</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">主材料</span></h3>
<p>白米 ···························2杯<br />
豆腐 ···························100g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a> ·······················300g<br />
香菇 ···························2朵<br />
银杏果 ·······················10粒<br />
葱花、黑芝麻 ·········各少许<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B8%83.md#1">昆布</a><ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E6%B1%A4.md#1">高汤</a> ··················300ml</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>调味料</strong></span></h3>
<p>酱油 ···························1大匙<br />
清酒 ···························1大匙<br />
胡椒 ···························少许<br />
葡萄籽油 ··················少许<br />
盐 ································少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B8%83.md#1">昆布</a>高汤作法</span></h3>
<p>煮锅饭时最常使用的高汤，和米饭很搭，能够增添鲜甜的滋味。</p>
<p>水500ml＋昆布1片(5cm×5cm)</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543921" style="width: 433px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543921-P22-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13543921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543921-P22-2.jpg" alt="" width="433" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543921" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13543922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543922" style="width: 434px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543922-P22-3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13543922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543922-P22-3.jpg" alt="" width="434" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543922" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水煮滚后放入昆布，煮约5分钟，待冷却后即可使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543930" style="width: 433px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543930-P22-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13543930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543930-P22-1.jpg" alt="" width="433" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543930" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1 .白米洗干净，用清水浸泡30分钟后沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543924-P47-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543924-P47-1-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543924" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.在鸡胸肉上倒入1大匙酱油、1大匙清酒、少许胡椒，混合均匀后静置腌渍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543925-P47-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543925-P47-2-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543925" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.豆腐切成1公分厚片，在平底锅上淋葡萄籽油、撒少许盐后，放入豆腐片煎到两面金黄上色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543926-P47-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543926-P47-3-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543926" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 .饭锅预热后淋少许油（材料分量外），放入腌好的鸡胸肉，两面稍微煎上色，再切成适合入口的大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543927-P47-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543927-P47-4-450x309.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543927" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 .煎过鸡胸肉的饭锅不用洗，直接放入浸泡过的米、倒入300ml昆布高汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543928-P47-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543928-P47-5-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543928" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 .放入鸡胸肉、香菇、银杏果、豆腐，盖锅盖，用大火煮5分钟至沸腾，再转小火加热10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543923-P46.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543923-P46-450x614.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="614" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543923" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/lpuzhr3267/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89%E9%94%85%E9%A5%AD.md#1">豆腐鸡胸肉锅饭</a>（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7 .接着关火，不开盖焖5分钟，再撒上少许盐及葱花、黑芝麻并用锅铲搅拌后，即可盛入碗中享用。</p>
<p><strong>TIP 腌渍：肉、鱼在料理前先用调味料等腌制入味，煮出来才会有味道。⊙</strong></p>
