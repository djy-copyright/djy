<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>避免高丽菜释出涩味 2诀窍留住香脆鲜甜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/shutterstock_571780078-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>高丽菜切法和调理方式的不同，会带出不同的风味。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>现在一年四季都可以看到<ahref="https://github.com/jxcpzp362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E4%B8%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">高丽菜</a>这个常备蔬菜，可是只要尝过冬天的高丽菜，你一定会发现，天哪！原来高丽菜也有如此可口美味的盛产季节呀。</p>
<p>占了这个时期八成产量、来自渥美半岛的冬季<ahref="https://github.com/jxcpzp362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E4%B8%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">高丽菜</a>，外观美，风味充满活力。或许是此地有暖流与太平洋吹拂的海风，这与高丽菜的故乡——地中海沿岸的风土气候非常相像，所以此地所产的高丽菜只要烫过，滋味会清甜得几乎不需要沙拉淋酱。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">切出可口美味高丽菜丝的诀窍</span></h2>
<p>很多人都以为高丽菜非常好烹调，而且几乎没有什么特殊风味。但是让我告诉大家，高丽菜可是非常倔强的。举例来说，本来想将高丽菜切成细丝，可是菜叶是不是就会莫名地硬到切不下去？汆烫的时候，是不是会不小心释出涩味呢？</p>
<p>烹调高丽菜时，诀窍是要注意到各个部位与纤维，可别以为这只不过是颗高丽菜喔！将纤维切断，然后再切成跟线一样细的高丽菜丝，风味绝对比平时的切法更美味万分。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">解体顺序</span></h2>
<p><strong>将菜心挖出，菜叶一片一片剥下。按照这个顺序比较不会撕破菜叶。</strong></p>
<p>1. <strong><span style="color: #800000;">剥下外叶</span></strong> ?按照卷的顺序，用手将2～3片的外叶从菜心处剥下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522198-0203-1205.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522198-0203-1205-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522198" class="wp-caption-text">按照高丽菜卷的顺序，用手将2～3片的外叶从菜心处剥下。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. <strong><span style="color: #800000;">切下菜轴</span> </strong>?切下菜轴，这样菜叶会比较好剥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522204" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522204-0203-1210.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522204-0203-1210-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522204" class="wp-caption-text">切下菜轴，这样菜叶会比较好剥。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-1 <span style="color: #800000;"><strong>挖除菜心</strong></span> ?刀尖刺入后转动菜刀，将菜心挖出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522205-0203-1215.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522205-0203-1215-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522205" class="wp-caption-text">刀尖刺入后转动菜刀，将菜心挖出。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-2 <strong><span style="color: #800000;">挖出菜心</span></strong> ?挖好的痕迹呈椭圆状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522206" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522206-0203-1216.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522206-0203-1216-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522206" class="wp-caption-text">挖好的痕迹呈椭圆状。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. <span style="color: #800000;"><strong>剥下菜叶</strong></span> ?按照菜叶卷的顺序一一剥下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522211-0203-1223.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522211-0203-1223-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522211" class="wp-caption-text">按照菜叶卷的顺序一一剥下。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. <strong><span style="color: #800000;">将中心部切开，剥除菜心</span></strong> ?将中心部切成两半， 剥除没有切下的菜心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522212" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522212-0203-1231.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522212-0203-1231-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522212" class="wp-caption-text">将中心部切成两半， 剥除没有切下的菜心。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">烹调技术</span></h2>
<p>沿着纤维，从叶脉之间切开会更甜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">基本烹调方式</span></h2>
<p>1. 切的时候留意纤维的方向，滋味会截然不同。</p>
<p>2. 用手撕成小块较不易释出涩味，风味也较好渗入。</p>
<p>3. 越往外侧的菜叶越适合用油烹调；越往内侧的菜叶则越适合生吃。</p>
<p>4. 加热至味道完全散去为止。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">切法</span></h2>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">顺着纤维切，味道清甜。将纤维切断，口感柔嫩。</span></strong></p>
<p>切的时候留意纤维方向，可以呈现不同的口感。<strong>切在叶脉上会释出涩味，因此菜刀要落在叶脉之间，顺着纤维切开，这是最基本的切法</strong>。如果要切成细丝或让口感更加柔嫩，则要将纤维切断。用手撕成小块，口感会较细腻温和，切面增加，烹调时会更容易入味。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">《用手从叶脉之间撕成小块》</span></strong></p>
<p>柔嫩的菜叶用手撕成小块，口感会比较好，且滋味甘甜。撕的时候要沿着叶脉。最后将叶脉折断。指尖用力，一点一点撕下即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522231" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522231-0318-5082.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522231-0318-5082-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522231" class="wp-caption-text">柔嫩的菜叶用手撕成小块，口感会比较好，且滋味甘甜。撕的时候要沿着叶脉。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13522232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522232-0318-5087.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522232-0318-5087-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522232" class="wp-caption-text">最后将叶脉折断。指尖用力，一点一点撕下即可。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">《切下菜心》</span></strong></p>
<p>1 将口感较硬的菜心切下。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>《从叶脉之间切开》</strong></span></p>
<p>2 将菜刀放在较粗的叶脉之间，沿着纤维切开，这样比较不容易释出涩味。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">《块状切法 大小一致》</span></strong></p>
<p>3 顺着叶脉切开后，再将纤维切断。大小切得一致，受热才会均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522236-0318-5058.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522236-0318-5058-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522236" class="wp-caption-text">大小切得一致，受热才会均匀。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">《丝状切法》</span></strong></p>
<p>中心部横切成圆盘状后，压住并从边端开始切丝。逆着纤维切，口感会比较柔软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522237" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522237-0318-5066.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522237-0318-5066-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522237" class="wp-caption-text">逆着纤维切，口感会比较柔软。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">《菜芯削成薄片》</span></strong></p>
<p>口感较硬的菜心用刀尖削成薄片。切面增加后不仅较易煮熟，也较能入味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522238" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522238-0318-5106.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522238-0318-5106-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522238" class="wp-caption-text">口感较硬的菜心用刀尖削成薄片。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">另一个解体的方法</span></h2>
<p>高丽菜上方（叶子外缘）与下方（菜心处）的厚度、纤维粗细、柔软度均不同。要切出粗细一致的高丽菜丝，最好分切成上下两半使用。呈圆盘状的中心部分，口感最为柔软，非常适合切成高丽菜丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522338-0318-5009.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522338-0318-5009-450x352.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522338" class="wp-caption-text">高丽菜上方（叶子外缘）与下方（菜心处）的特质厚度、纤维、软度都不同，最好分成两部分处理。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 剥下3～4片外叶，将菜轴</p>
<p>2. 将内侧分切成上下两半。切落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522339-0318-5004.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522339-0318-5004-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522339" class="wp-caption-text">将内侧分切成上下两半。切落。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 取出中心部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13522340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13522340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522340-0318-5019.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13522340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522340-0318-5019-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13522340" class="wp-caption-text">取出中心部分。（爱米粒出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
